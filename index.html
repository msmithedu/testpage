Biography

Ms. Smith has been an educator for over 3 years, specializing in ancient history and social studies. She holds a Bachelor's Degree in Education from ISU and is currently working on her masters.

Teaching Philosophy

Ms. Smith believes in an immersive and interactive approach to teaching. She encourages students to explore ancient civilizations through hands-on projects, discussions, and field trips, fostering a deep appreciation for history.
